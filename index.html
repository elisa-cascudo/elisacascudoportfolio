About Me

Elisa Cascudo
Data Analyst

It all started with a bang: I quit Engineering after two years to pursue Corporate Communication. I'd fallen in love with the power of meaning. With how people converge, so often in serendipity, and create whole new possibilities for our world, just using language. Written language, spoken word, visual signs and...

Math and coding? *crickets*

Those two were missing from my curricula. I did three years of Chinese, learned the psychological impact of of sounds and colors, how one word in a sentence could set off international conflicts... and yet, not a single line of code.

That's how I ended up in a Masters Degree of Decision Making Systems (URJC), learning statistics, probability, R, prediction models... all the good stuff to make better decisions. To create better possibilities for our world.

Every corner of this website is for you. Maybe we'll work together. Maybe you'll learn something new. Maybe you'll just smile.

All is welcome here!
